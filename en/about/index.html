About

omemo believes that a beautiful life stems from the emotional connections we create and the pursuit of balance across three key areas: spirit, health, and wealth. We are dedicated to crafting joyful user experiences that not only help you connect with cherished memories of the past but also empower you to create precious moments for the future. At the spiritual level, omemo launched the oThings memory-keeping app, enabling you to focus on noting your loved things. On the wealth front, omemo introduced the oSold POS app, designed to assist you in cashing in on your dreams and turning your passions into reality.

Founder#

Kyle Lei

Kyle Lei began his design career in 2010, firmly believing that design is a discipline that combines science and philosophy to improve people’s well-being. Recognizing that technology shapes modern life, he has continually refined his design skills alongside technological advancements, evolving from industrial design to user interface design. As artificial intelligence became a driving force in technology, Kyle gained a deeper understanding of the inseparable connection between design feasibility and technological creativity. This realization motivated him to self-learn programming, leading him to propose the concept of a hybrid experience design that integrates both design and software development. In 2023, he founded the brand omemo, showcasing his creative thinking in merging business, design, and programming.
