oThings

Note your loved things#

Your life experience is unique, and your uniqueness is reflected in your loved things. These things may be your collections, keepsakes, or precious memories of events. However, as time passes and things accumulate, the memories of these things may gradually fade away.

oThing is a memory-keeping application designed specifically for your life memories, allowing you to focus on noting your loved things.

Note your collections, keepsakes, or precious memories of events with oThings. It offers comprehensive metadata, multi-currency records, and tag markings to capture the uniqueness of your loved things and the depth of their connection to your life. Showcase your taste in life through diverse categorization and layouts.
